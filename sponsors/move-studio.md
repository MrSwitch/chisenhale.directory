Your friendly local studio.. move is a female-founded and fully independent studio offering Yoga, Fitness, Barre and Pilates to bring movement and wellbeing into our community. We have classes for all levels with a diverse team of experienced instructors, and if you’re not sure where to start then you can reach out anytime and we’ll be happy to talk you through your options.

We want to encourage movement of all kinds, for all people. We have found that the joy and the freedom of regular movement has been invaluable in our own lives and we want to share that passion with you. We hope to bring our community together and help to promote health and wellbeing in the often chaotic pace of London life.
