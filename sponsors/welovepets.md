We Love Pets Hackney is a welfare-first pet care services across Hackney, Bow, Homerton and the surrounding areas. Led by Chisenhale parent Michelle, the team is passionate about delivering safe, reliable and personalised care for every pet we look after.

We offer professional dog walking, doggy daycare and home boarding, pet sitting, puppy care, cat sitting and small animal care, tailored around your pet’s individual needs and routine. 

As genuine animal lovers and pet owners ourselves, the We Love Pets Hackney team understands how important it is to find someone you can fully trust. So whether you need a regular walk to help you while you work or someone to look after your pets when you’re away, we’re here to help - get in touch!
