Play. Create. Discover. For 0-11 year olds and their grown-ups.

At the heart of the centre, purpose-built Story World and Story Garden are creative play spaces designed to inspire children’s curiosity and imagination. Changing annually, interactive exhibitions immerse families in the worlds of their favourite authors and illustrators, from Michael Rosen to Julia Donaldson and Axel Scheffler to Judith Kerr. 

Our Story Builders lead interactive exhibition sessions and 0-3s Storytelling, and pop up in the Story Worlds and Story Garden to tell more tales.  

Throughout the year, much-loved and up-and-coming authors, illustrators and other artists run exciting events.
