Love to Swim is an award-winning swim school offering small-group lessons (max 5 per class) for children across East London. Our expert teachers follow the Swim England Learn to Swim Programme, helping children build water confidence and strong swimming skills in a fun, supportive environment.

We also offer adult lessons, ladies-only sessions, and specialist Autism-friendly classes, supporting swimmers of all ages and abilities.

Spaces are limited.

New families receive 15% off a 6-lesson block with code: CHISENHALE15
