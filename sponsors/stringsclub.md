AWARD-WINNING HOLIDAY CAMPS IN BOW FOR CHILDREN AGED 4-11

Looking for trusted, engaging and screen-free Holiday Camps in Bow?

The Strings Club offers award-winning, 5★ rated Holiday Camps at Chisenhale Primary School, blending the joys and benefits of music with creative, interactive and screen-free experiences children genuinely love.

Founded by musician and mum Amy Cunningham, our camps are designed to help children build confidence, make friendships and create joyful childhood memories during the school holidays.

Each day includes:

• Interactive guitar, violin or ukulele sessions

• Carefully-curated workshops including art, drama, wellbeing and creative projects

• Energetic games and outdoor activities

• Opportunities to learn new skills, explore creativity and connect with others

Why families choose The Strings Club:

• Feefo Platinum Trusted Service Award Winner 2026

• Proudly rated 'Above Standard' in our three most recent Ofsted inspections

• Over 500 verified 5★ reviews from families

• Ofsted-registered childcare

• Accepts all major childcare vouchers and Tax-Free Childcare

• Free instrument hire for children attending more than one day

• Daily parent updates and private photo galleries

• Flexible booking options, from a single day to a full week

The Strings Club is proud to be part of the Buy Women Built community and has inspired almost half a million children through the joys and benefits of music since 2012.
