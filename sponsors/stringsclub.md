Looking for a holiday experience that’s creative, engaging and completely screen-free? Welcome to The Strings Club - the award-winning, music-led Holiday Camps where children build confidence, make friendships and have an unforgettable time through music, creativity and play.
Founded by mum and musician Amy Cunningham, The Strings Club is loved by families across London, the Midlands and Warwickshire for its warm, nurturing approach and exciting mix of musical learning and creative activities. Every camp is designed to help children unplug from devices, express themselves freely and thrive in a fun, supportive environment.
Through our carefully designed activities, children develop:
• Confidence through creativity and self-expression
• Meaningful friendships and a sense of belonging
• Imagination, curiosity and independence
• Focus, resilience and emotional wellbeing - all nurtured through music
